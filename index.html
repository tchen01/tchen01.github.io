Tyler Chen (陳泰樂)

I am a Courant Instructor at New York University, co-appointed by Mathematics at Courant and Computer Science and Engineering at Tandon. Previously, I graduated from the University of Washington with a PhD in applied mathematics and from Tufts University with degrees in math and physics and a minor in studio art.
