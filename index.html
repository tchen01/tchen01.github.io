research
I do research with the softmattertheory group at tufts
I'm currently working to optimize a monte carlo simulation used to explore the behavior of balls constrained to the surfaces of ellipsoids under various conditions. By exploiting the nature of the particle-particle interactions and geometric constraints I was able to rewrite write a linear time n-body algorithm for our simulations. My algorithm reduces the runtime for a large number of particles by over a factor of 50 compared to the original quadratic time algorithm.
